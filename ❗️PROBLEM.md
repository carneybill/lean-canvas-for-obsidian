# Problem

Our target customers struggle with **inefficient workflows**, **lack of clarity in business planning**, and **difficulty aligning teams around a shared vision**.

1. **Inefficient Workflows** – Many entrepreneurs and small teams rely on scattered tools (spreadsheets, notes, whiteboards) to plan their business models, leading to confusion and lost information.
2. **Lack of Clarity** – Without a structured framework, it’s hard to identify what truly drives value or where the biggest risks lie.
3. **Team Misalignment** – Teams often fail to stay on the same page about goals, assumptions, and priorities.

## Existing Alternatives

Currently, people use tools like Notion, Google Docs, or Excel templates to create Lean Canvases. However, these lack automation, integration, and guided insights that help users refine their ideas effectively.
