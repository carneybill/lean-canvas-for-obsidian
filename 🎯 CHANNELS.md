# Channels

Our primary channels for reaching and engaging customers are designed to meet creators, entrepreneurs, and small business owners where they already are.

1. **Obsidian Community** – Publish on the Obsidian forum, Discord, and plugin marketplace to reach active users.
2. **Content Marketing** – Create tutorials, YouTube videos, and blog posts showing how to use Lean Canvas for Obsidian effectively.
3. **Partnerships** – Collaborate with productivity influencers and startup coaches.
4. **Email & Social Media** – Build a newsletter and share updates on X (Twitter), Reddit, and LinkedIn.
5. **Word of Mouth** – Encourage users to share their canvases and workflows publicly.
