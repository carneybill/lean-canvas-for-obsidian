# Solution

To address the key problems identified, our solution provides a **smart, guided Lean Canvas experience** within Obsidian that helps entrepreneurs and teams plan, validate, and iterate their business ideas efficiently.

1. **Integrated Canvas System** – All Lean Canvas sections are linked markdown files, allowing seamless navigation and updates.
2. **AI Assistance** – Automatically generates draft content for each section based on user input, saving time and improving clarity.
3. **Collaboration Ready** – Enables teams to share and refine ideas in real time using Obsidian’s sync and version control.
4. **Insight Generation** – Provides AI-driven suggestions to identify weak points, assumptions, and opportunities for improvement.
