# Cost Structure

Our cost structure focuses on maintaining a lean, sustainable model while supporting development, marketing, and community engagement.

## Fixed Costs
- **Development Tools** – Software licenses, hosting, and infrastructure.
- **AI API Usage** – Costs associated with AI model integration.
- **Marketing & Branding** – Website, content creation, and design.
- **Community Management** – Support and moderation for user engagement.

## Variable Costs
- **Plugin Maintenance** – Updates, bug fixes, and feature improvements.
- **Affiliate & Partner Programs** – Commissions for referrals and collaborations.
- **Transaction Fees** – Payment processing for premium features or donations.
