
# Revenue Streams

Our revenue model is designed to balance accessibility for individual users with sustainability for long-term development.

## Pricing Model
- **Free Tier** – Core Lean Canvas functionality available to all users.
- **Pro Tier** – Premium features such as AI-assisted generation, advanced analytics, and export options.
- **Consulting & Workshops** – Offer personalized sessions for startups and teams to optimize their Lean Canvases.
- **Community Support** – Optional donations or sponsorships from the Obsidian community.

## Revenue Sources
1. Subscription fees from Pro users.
2. One-time purchases for add-ons or templates.
3. Affiliate partnerships with startup tools and services.
4. Sponsorships and community contributions.
