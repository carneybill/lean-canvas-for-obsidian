# Key Metrics

To measure success and ensure continuous improvement, we track the following key metrics:

1. **Active Users** – Number of users actively using the Lean Canvas for Obsidian plugin.
2. **Canvas Completions** – How many users complete all sections of their Lean Canvas.
3. **Engagement Rate** – Frequency of updates or edits made to canvases.
4. **AI Usage Frequency** – How often users invoke AI assistance for content generation.
5. **Conversion Rate** – Percentage of users who move from free to premium features (if applicable).
6. **User Retention** – Percentage of users returning after 30 days.
7. **Community Growth** – Number of mentions, shares, or contributions from the Obsidian community.
