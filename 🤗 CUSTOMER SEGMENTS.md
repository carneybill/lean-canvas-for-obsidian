
# Customer Segments

Our primary customers are **entrepreneurs, creators, and small business teams** who want to plan and validate their business ideas efficiently.

## Target Customers

1. **Solo Founders** – Individuals building startups or side projects who need a structured way to plan.
2. **Small Teams** – Early-stage teams collaborating on business models and product-market fit.
3. **Consultants & Coaches** – Professionals helping clients develop business strategies.

## Early Adopters

- Active Obsidian users who value local-first tools.
- Startup founders familiar with Lean Canvas or Business Model Canvas.
- Productivity enthusiasts who enjoy structured thinking and note-based workflows.
